231115 \ Hello World! \ new website, new blog

    Hello there I thought it would be a bit of fun to start my own website - so here I am! This week I am working towards my first crit at the Bartlett, having had my first pin up a couple of weeks ago. I have stumbled across a rather brilliant Cedric Price project which is similar to the project I am working on - of creating a new form of governance building in Marseille. This project by Cedric price is called 'Pop-up Parliament' and I went to the British Library to sift through the 1965 volume of bound New Society magazines to find it. Here is the premise:
      to knock down parliament's existing buildings in london and build a pop up parliament building - see picture below
      and this building would then offer a new flexible, more democratic architecture to enable a more transparent governance. This has been a great help for formulating my own programme - so time will tell what spaces my own design will hold! Kx
