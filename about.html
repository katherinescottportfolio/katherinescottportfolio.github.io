about me

academic history

    Currently undertaking RIBA part II studies at UCL Bartlett, in unit 21 under Abigail Ashton, Andrew Porter and Tom Holberton. This year's brief is set in Marseilles - the gateway to the mediterranean from europe. Starting with Jean Claude-Izzo's 'Total Kheops' and now looking at modern governance, I have now begun designing my first postgrad design project.
    Before this, I studied at university of nottingham for undergraduate RIBA part I - and graduated with a first class honours in BArch Architecture.

employment history

    I worked at Hawkins\Brown for 13 months, focused largely on Infrastructure, in particular the Crossrail Liverpool St Station project. I also worked on a higher education bid and some public participation work for a community project.
    I have also worked freelance, doing drawings for a residential extension.
    I have prior work experience as an intern at CZWG and Knight Architects also.
    Other roles have included being a UCAS rep at the University of Nottingham and as a waitress for several years at Hazlemere Golf Club.
